Join GS1 Kenya & get barcodes

To attain GS1 barcodes for your business you will need to join as a member. By becoming a member you have access to our unique range of barcode numbers, support, training and much more.

Are you already a GS1 member?

Contact the member service desk to assist you with:

  • Getting more numbers (GTINs,GLNs,Additional Prefix)

  • Update company information such as turnover, contacts e.t.c

  • Request for training and consultancy


Are you new to GS1?

This is what you will need to provide:

  • Your company certificate of registration or incorporation

  • KRA pin Certificate

  • For Sole Proprietorships, also attach I.D.

  • List of products you would want to begin bar-coding
