Membership Benefits

GS1 as a member organization has a history that spans back more than 40 years. The members cut across various industries utilizing GS1 Standards to enable seamless business operations.

What does membership entail?

As a member driven organization, GS1 Kenya requires one to join as a member before they are allocated and licensed to use a company prefix which acts as a building block of GS1 Standards.

As a GS1 Kenya member, you will have access to a wide range of tools, expertise, and support to help you as your business grows.

Member Support

  • Member support team accesible through Email and Telephone.
  • Our advisory team provides guidance on matters including barcode quality as well as implementations issues

Tools & Resources to get you started

  • Case studies on success stories.
  • Industry specific trainings and workshops
  • Industry specific guidleines and education materials

What's in it for your organization?

  • Adopt global potential by using standards acceptable anywhere in the world.
  • Enhance supply chain processes and performance.
  • Leverage the use of innovative supply chain solutions.

Other Membership Benefits

  • Ongoing use of unique GS1 barcode numbers
  • A wide range of complimentary (or discounted) training and support
  • Complimentary and discounted barcode verification
  • Member pricing on all GS1 Kenya services
  • Access to best practice information and standards for supply chain management
  • Gateway Magazine – our magazine offers members free updates and information on the GS1 standards and developments country wide, regionally and internationally.
